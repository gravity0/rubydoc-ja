instance method Complex#denominator

denominator -> Fixnum[permalink][rdoc]

自分自身の実部・虚部の分母のLCM(最小公倍数)を返します。

例: 

z1 = Complex.new(1, 2)
z2 = Complex.new(Rational.new!(1, 3), Rational.new!(3, 5))

p z1.denominator  #=> 1
p z2.denominator  #=> 15
